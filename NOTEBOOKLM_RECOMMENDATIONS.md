## üîÑ Alternative Approach: Individual Audios

If you have **NotebookLM Plus** and want more detail:

### Variant A: 12 Short Audios

Create **separate audio for each manual** (Shorter - 5 min).

**Pros:**
- Quick overview of each material's unique content
- Can choose which to deep dive later

**Cons:**
- 12 audios is a lot
- May have repetition
- Harder to get big picture

### Variant B: 2 Large Audios

**Audio 1:** All small manuals (under 1MB) together
**Audio 2:** All large manuals (over 1MB) together

**Pros:**
- Simpler
- Two comprehensive overviews

**Cons:**
- May be too general
- Harder to navigate by topic

## üí° Tips for People with ADHD

### Structure is Your Friend
- Listen to audios **in order** (1 ‚Üí 2 ‚Üí 3 ‚Üí 4)
- Don't try to listen to everything at once
- Take breaks as needed

### Note Taking
- Play NotebookLM in background
- Take notes on key points
- Use NotebookLM interactive mode for questions

### Repetition
- Audios are downloadable - you can re-listen later
- If something wasn't clear, ask NotebookLM for clarification

## ‚öôÔ∏è Technical Info

### Uploading Files to NotebookLM

1. Go to NotebookLM (notebooklm.google.com)
2. Create new Notebook
3. Upload recommended PDFs from `originaalid/` folder
4. Click **Customize** (not Generate!)
5. Enter customize prompt
6. Choose length
7. Click **Generate**

### If File is Too Large

**Support Groups Queensland** (4.9MB) may be too large.

**Solution:**
- Try uploading - if it doesn't work, leave it out of Audio 4
- Or use only that one file for separate audio
